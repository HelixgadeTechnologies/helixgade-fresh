We know you have Questions

We are tailored to work with start-up founders and Venture partners looking to launch a product or rebrand to expand into new markets. If you have a vision you want to bring to life, “Helixgade” is for you.

You'll have a dedicated Account Manager throughout our time together who will be on hand to address any queries, provide insights, and ensure the smooth execution of your vision from ideation to launch.

Different projects have different scopes and timelines but we pride ourselves on efficient delivery, and we'll provide a clear timeline at the outset to set expectations.

Our process typically involves an initial consultation to gather project details and understand your goals. we then proceed to research, ideation, and design iterations, working closely with you for feedback and approvals. our aim is to ensure a collaborative and transparent process throughout the project's lifecycle. Consistent and open communication is at the heart of our partnership. We utilise a mix of scheduled check-ins, email updates, and Slack for easy collaboration, to ensure you're always in the loop and can provide feedback at every stage.

Yes, we value long-term relationships with our clients and offer post-project support and maintenance services. whether you need assistance with updates, troubleshooting, or further enhancements, we are here to support you beyond the project's completion.
