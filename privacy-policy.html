Privacy Policy

Helixgade ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website [https://helixgade.com/]. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as your name, email address, and phone number when you sign up. Additionally, we may collect information automatically through cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to provide services, personalize your experience on our site, and communicate with you about our products and services. We may also use aggregated data for analytical purposes to improve our offerings. The legal basis for processing your personal data includes your consent, the necessity to perform a contract with you, compliance with legal obligations, and our legitimate interests.

3. Sharing Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties unless necessary for fulfilling services you request. We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, provided they agree to keep this information confidential. We ensure all third-party processors comply with applicable data protection laws.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We keep your personal information only as long as necessary to provide you with our services and for legitimate business purposes. If you request, we will delete or anonymize your personal information unless we are legally required to retain certain information.

6. Cookies

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, remember your preferences, and analyze how our website is used. You can control cookies through your browser settings and other tools. For detailed information on the cookies we use and how to manage your preferences, please refer to our cookie policy.

7. Your Rights

Under U.S. privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify any incorrect or incomplete data
  • Erase your personal data
  • Restrict processing of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at helixgade@gmail.com.

8. Data Protection officer

If applicable, our Data Protection Officer can be contacted at helixgade@gmail.com for any data protection concerns.

9. International Data Transfers

Your personal information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We take steps to ensure your data is protected in compliance with applicable data protection laws during such transfers.

10. Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal data, we will notify you within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, in accordance with applicable laws.

11. Updates to this Privacy Policy

From time to time, we may update this privacy policy to reflect new services or changes. All changes made will be posted on this page, and where changes will materially affect you, we will notify you of this change by placing a notice on our website or by email.
